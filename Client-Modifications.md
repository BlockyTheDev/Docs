Some nice Minecraft client mod developers allow us to control their mod over a hidden motd message.

**Currently NoCheatPlus supports:**
* CJB Mods
* Rei's Minimap
* Smart Moving
* Zombe's modpack
* JourneyMap

If you have another mod you want us to support then please contact us.
